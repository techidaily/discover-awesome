## How to Remove Audio from a Video on Mac?

iMovie is a free video editing application designed for Mac users with little or no video editing experience. The software is quite simple to operate and includes various effects and features for making professional home movies. With iMovie, you can easily trim, merge, change the speed, add and remove audio from videos, and achieve more.

Here is how to remove audio from video clip in iMovie on Mac:

_Tips. iMovie is pre-installed on a Mac computer. If you don‚Äôt have the app, download it from the Mac App Store first._

**Step 1.** Open iMovie and click ‚ÄúProjects‚Äù > ‚ÄúCreate New‚Äù to create a new project.  
**Step 2.** Click the down arrow icon (or press ‚ÄúCommand + I‚Äù) to import a video from your computer. Next, drag the video to the timeline.  
**Step 3.** Select the video and right-click it. Then, choose ‚ÄúDetach Audio‚Äù from the popup window.  
**Step 4.** After that, right-click the audio track and select ‚ÄúDelete‚Äù to remove sound from the video.  
**Step 5.** Finally, click the ‚ÄúShare‚Äù button in the upper right corner and tap ‚ÄúFile‚Äù to export the video.
